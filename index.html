About CultureConnect NG

CultureConnect NG is a web platform dedicated to showcasing Nigerian cultural heritage through art, music, and stories while providing a space for young creatives to collaborate, learn, and share their work. Led by a team of passionate individuals committed to cultural preservation, the platform fosters a vibrant creative ecosystem.

Key Features

  • Interactive galleries showcasing Nigerian art and music.
  • A forum for creatives to connect and collaborate.
  • Free tutorials for youth to learn digital and creative skills.
  • A blog highlighting local cultural stories and traditions.
